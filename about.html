About Our Mission

Preserving traditional Christian music and bringing spiritual resources to communities worldwide

Who We Are

This question may arise in the minds of many especially those visiting this site for the first time. We are a group of people from different denominations who believe traditional music and hymns in various languages should be accessible to everyone everywhere in the world, regardless of their location.

Our mission is to preserve cultural worship traditions while making spiritual resources accessible to all through modern technology.

Our Journey

Worshipping Through Music was created in 2022 to make hymns and traditional music available to everyone. We started on YouTube where hymns and traditional music in various languages (Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga, English) were published. Later, we launched this website to expand our offerings with hymn books in both soft and hard copies, as well as Bibles.

What We Do

As worshippingthroughmusic.com, we share music hymns, traditional music, and books like The Bible and The Hymn Book. Our platform is designed so users can easily switch between hymns/music videos and written hymns, experiencing both written and audible worship.

How We Work

We have a YouTube channel where traditional hymns and music videos by different churches are uploaded weekly in various playlists. These videos are accessible through links on both YouTube and our website worshippingthroughmusic.com.

Our platform is designed so users can easily switch between hymns/music videos and written hymns, experiencing both written and audible worship in one seamless experience.

Our Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to the spiritual nourishment of traditional worship music, regardless of their location or background. By preserving these cultural treasures and making them accessible through modern technology, we aim to:

  • Bridge generational gaps in worship practices
  • Connect diverse Christian communities
  • Provide spiritual resources to remote areas
  • Preserve cultural worship traditions
  • Create a global community of worshippers
