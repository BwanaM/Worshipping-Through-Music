About Us

Do you want to know about us? If you have any interest in us and wanted to know the motive behind the launch of this worshippingthroughmusic.com website. Then you are on the right page. On this page, you can find out all of the answers to your questions, what you want to know. So, without wasting much of your valuable time, let us delve into our topic of discussion.

Who are we?

This question may arise in the minds of many especially those visiting this site for the first time, who are we? We are now sitting down to write the answers to all those questions to remove all the doubts in their minds. We are a group of people from different Denominations who feel traditional music and Hymns done in different languages can be made available to everyone everywhere in the world regardless of their locations, as a way of spreading the good news.

What do we do?

As worshippingthroughmusic.com we share Music Hymns, Traditional Music and Books like The Bible and The Hymn Book.

How do we work?

We have a YouTube channel where traditional and Hymns music videos by different churches are uploaded weekly in various play lists. These videos are shown through the links on both YouTube and our website worshippingthroughmusic.com

What is our Goal?

Worshipping Through Music was created in 2022 in order to make Hymns and other types of traditional Music available to everyone. We first started on YouTube where Hymns and traditional Music in various languages amongst them Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga and English where published. It was later made public through its website worshippingthroughmusic.com where various types of initiatives are being introduced. Noticeable amongst these are the sale of Hymn Books in both soft and Hard Copies and also the sale of Bibles.

Statement

Worshippingthroughmusic.com is designed in a way that users are able to switch between Hymns/Other Music Videos and written hymns with easy. In such a way users can experience both written and audible various music
