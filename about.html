About

Worshipping Through Music was created in 2022 in order to make Hymns and other types of traditional Music available to everyone.
It first started on YouTube where Hymns and traditional Music in various languages amongst them Bemba, Nyanja, Tonga and English where published.
It was later made public through its website worshippingthroughmusic.com where various types of initiatives are being introduced.
Noticeable amongst these are the sale of Hymn Books in both soft and Hard Copies and also the sale of Bibles.
