62. YESU BALIMUSULILE

"Hail! Thou once despised Jesus"

1. Yesu, balimusulile,

'Mfumu ya ku Galili

Mwaculile, tupusuke,

Ni mwe mwatulubwile,

Mwe Katula wa bucushi.

Mwasende nsoni shesu,

Muli imwe twalishuka.

'Shina lyenu lya mweo

2. E lambo Lesa 'lisonta,

Fibi fyesu kusenda,

Luse e Iwamutumine,

Lwa kutuwikishanya.

'Bantu benu baelelwa,

Mulandu wa mulopa.

Icipata caisulwa

Kuti bonse bengile.

3. Yesu muli mu lulumbi

Ukwikalilila pe;

Abatulwa bamutasha,

Ku cipuna kwa Shifwe

Uko muletulombela,

E cifulo twapelwa.

Pe fye muletupepela

Tusuke tukafike.

4 'Mucinshi lulumbya maka,

Fya kulinga tupele;

Amalumbo yashakapwe,

Yatwamino kuleta;

Mutwafwe, mwe ba kutulwa

Ne nyimbo shawamisha;

Twimbile Umupusushi,

Tulumbanye 'Manyule.
