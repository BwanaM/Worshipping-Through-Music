88. CILONGANINO CABA

"The Church's one foundation"

1. Cilonganino caba

Pali Yesu Kristu,

Cane cilengo cipya

Ca shiwi na menshi

Aishile cifwaya,

Ukuba nabwinga,

Cashitwa no mulopa,

Alicifwilile.

2. Baitwa bena fyalo

Ku kufyalwa cipya,

'Bucetekelo bumo

Ne mfumu imo fye;

Citashe shina limo

Cilyo cimo cilya,

Ubusubuli bumo

Ebo cakwatisha.

3. Mu ncito no bulanda

,

Pamo na mu bulwi;

Cilindilo mutende

Uwa ciyayaya;

'Lintu ifya lulumbi

Fikesa moneka,

Elyo cilonganino

Kuti calatusha.

4. Calumdana panonse

Na Lesa patatu,

Cilomfwana na balya

Abaletusho 'ko,

Mwe banga, mwe bashila!

Mwe Mfumu tupeni

Pamo nabwa bafuke

Tukekale nemwe.
