223. KWAFYALILWE KULI FWE

"Unto us a Son is Born"

1. kwafyalilwe kuli fwe

Mfumu ya fyalo fyonse

Ku kupususha bantu

Afumine ku mulu.

2. Mu mukonda alele

Umo finama fyaba

Lelo filya fyamwene

Umwana wa bupapo.

3. Shamfumu sha panonse

Shali no bufuba,

Shaipeye 'baice

Abali mu Betle'mu.

4. Mwana uyu aishile

Kale mu kutemwa,

Awamya imitima

No lubuto lwa cine,

5. We mwana wakwa Mary,

Mututungulule

Mu nshila ya panonse

Na ku mwesu ku mulu.

6. Aleluya mwe Mwana

Mfumu ya kutemwa!

Twapunda no buseko

Ululumbi, lulumbi.
