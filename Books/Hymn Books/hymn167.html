167. TWAISA, TWAISA M' NGANDA

1. Twaisa, twaisa, m' nganda yenu

Kupepa imwe, mwe Mfumu.

2. Iseni, iseni kuli ifwe;

Tulelindilila mwe Bene.

3. Mubule. mubue ukutota,

Tuletotela imwe pano.

4. Umfweni, umfweni, mwe Shikulu,

Ifyo twalalomba ku Mfumu.

5. Tulomba, tulomba, shi mufushe

be bumba lyenu ilya panshi.

6. Bakeni, bakeni, ifwee bonse

Pa kweshiwa ku fibi fyonse.

7. Fwe babi mutusambe;

Ifilonda fyesu muposhe.

8. Tubute, tubute imitima,

Tupashanye Yesu ubusuma.

9. Twafweni, twafweni, mwe Shikulu.

Tutemwe imwe Lesa M'kulu.
