191. NOMBA PA KWINGILA

"Standing at the portal"

1. Nomba pa kwingila

Ku mwaka upya,

'Mashiwi yawama

Yatukosesha.

'Shiwi lya kwa Tata,

Lyatusosesha;

Ishiwi lya maka

Lyatusekesha.

Twende no kushipa

'Bana ba Mfumu,

Pantwi shiwi lyakwe

Libelelela.

2. Lesa ali ne fwe

Ifyo asosa,

Alatwafwa lyonse,

Teti tutine.

"Ine ndemubaka,

'Kuboko kwandi

Kulemufishisha.

Ku cinso candi."

3. 'Mwaka uletampa

Mu luse lwakwe,

Bonse bakamona

Fya bupe fyakwe

Babi na balanda

Balwelwe pamo

Kuli aba bonse

Kulyu bupalo.

4. Teti atulabe,

Nangu kutusha,

Pantu alaile

'kuba na ifwe.

Tumucetekele

'Mwako no wine,

Lesa ali ne fwe

'Nshiku shonse pe.
