19. TULUMBANYE LESA

1. Twalumbanye Lesa

No tunwa ne mitima

Kalenga wa maka

Shifwe uwatutemwa

E watukushishe

Kufuma ku bwaice

E utusunga pe

Kufika lelo.

2. Mwe Lesa waluse

Tusungeni mupepi

Mutwenshe mu nshila

Yenu iya mutende

Mutupelye maka

Ya kwansha Satana

Tusuke tufike

Ku bufumu bwenu

3. Tutotele Lesa

Shifwe, Mwana, mupashi,

Balumbwe panonse

Baleteka mu mulu

Lesa umo wine

Kabumba wa fyonse

Ku kale ne lelo

Inshiku shonse.
