171. LESA MUTUPALE

“Lord , bless us and pity us”

1. Lesa, mutupale

Mube no luse pe

Lekeni mpumi ya Mfumu

Itubalikile.

2. Calo cishibishe,

Ne mikowa yonsu,

Cikuku cenu cisuma

Ciletula bonse.

3. Bacindike imwe,

Bantu bacindike;

Mikowa isekelele,

Bonse bamwimbile.

4. Imwe mupingule

Mwalilungamisha;

Muteke bantu mu cine,

Bonse ba mikowa.

5. Bantu bamilumbe,

Bamulumbe bonse;

Banono na bakalmba

Batashe imwe pe.

6. Calo catatwala;

Lesa ‘tume palo;

Abantu balamutina

Ku mpela sha calo.
