125. BONSE NATULUMBE LESU WESU

"Saviour, like a shepherd lead us"

1. Bonse natulumbe Lesa wesu

'Luse atubelela;

Tumutemwe pantu atutemwa,

'Buku lyakwe lyatweba.

Yesu mwine, aishile

Kuno twaba fwe bantu;

Ukufwayo kupususha

Fwe bonse balubile.

2. Bonse fwaisheni Lesa wesu,

Pantu akulatwafwa;

Calilembwa Lesa aleumfwa

Bonse balepalama.

3. Bonse subileni Lesa wesu,

Emo tuleshukila;

Alefwaya tumucetekele,

Iyi nshila isuma.
