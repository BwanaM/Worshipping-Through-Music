146. MWE YESU MWALINTEMWA

"Jesus, Lover of my soul"

1. Mwe Yesu mwalintemwa,

Ni kui 'imwe ndeisa;

Ico 'mwela waima,

Yampuma na mabimbi;

Mfiseni mwe Katula,

Ku fikali fya panshi,

Nkamone 'Pusukilo,

Ku mwenu 'ko ku mulu.

2. Kafwa umbi nshikwete,

Kani imwe, Shikulu:

Mwinsha fye, napapata,

Mulensunga nshiku pe.

Ndemucetekela pe,

Muli Katula Wandi,

'Mutwe wandi mucinge

Mu cintelelwe cenu.

3. Kristu ndwmufwaisha,

Nimwe mweo na maka,

Muleimya abafwa,

Mwe muti wa balwala,

Shina lyenu lisuma,

Ine muntu wabipa,

Njisula no bubi,

Mwa cine ne cikuku.

4. 'Luse lwenu lwafula,

Lwakunsamba ne mubi,

Nsambeni no ku mbaka,

Mbe wa cine ne subi,

Nimwe kamfuku,

Nwenshepo mweo wape,

Fukauka muli 'ne

Mweo ushipelela.
