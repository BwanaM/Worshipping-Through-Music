181. YESU ALETWITA BONSE

“Yesus calls us o’er the tumult”

1. Yesu aletwita bonse,

Twime, twende, tukonke;

Kwi shiwi lyanaka ‘leti,

“Mwina Kristu, unkonke,"

2. Ifyo baumfwa na kale

Kuli bemba Galili,

Baleke fyonse fya panshi,

Batemwe mfumu iyi.

3. Aletwita nefwe lelo,

Ati, “Fyafye wiyesha,

Wilolela fya mu calo;

Mwina Kristu ntemwisha.”

4. Ku kutusha no kubomba,

Ku kucula no kwanga,

Aisako, aletweba,

Muntemwe no musamwe.

5. Aletwita; mwe Ba luse,

Muletwafwo kwitaba,

Tupe mitima, tuteshe,

No kubomba kuli mwe.
