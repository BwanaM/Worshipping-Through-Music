207. BA KWA LESA BAFUMINE

"When from Egypt's house"

1. Ba kwa Lesa bafumine

Mu Egupti mu busha

Bali fye na bana pamo,

Balalola pasuma;

Abaice

Baleenda mu mpanga.

2. Bayawike na yordani,

Bafika ku kanaani,

Baikele umutende,

Ne fisuma bamwene

Aba bana

Mu nyimbo batotela.

3. Mwe katula, nefwe tube

Abalendo nga balya;

Imwe mwalitulubula

Ku fibi na ku ciwa,

Nomba nefwe

Twende ne mwe mu mpanga.

4. Yesu, mututungulule;

Mucinge ku kasuba;

Isuke mfifi ipite,

Mubutushe inshila;

Mututule

Kuli fyonse fyabipa.

<

5. Nga twafika pa mumana

watala wa mfifi,

Mube naifwe mu memshi,

Mutwabusho mutende;

Mufumye po

Umwenso wesu onse.

6. Ilyo lwendo lwesu lwapwa,

Tukamona pasuma;

Tukekala na bashila,

Abana ba mu ng'anda,

Fwe bashuka.

Tukalatasha Lesa.
