110. NI KATULA, PA MFWA YAKWE

Ni katula, pa mfwa yakwe, abulile umukate ati,

Ndemupela, ndemupela umubili, umubili wandi

Mwe buleni, mwe buleni umukate, umukate, mulye

Ibukisha, ibukisha, ukutemwa, ukutemwa, kwandi

Abulile, abulile, ulukomba. ulukombo, ati

E mulopa, e mulopa wasumine, wasumine imwe.

Ilayano, ilayano, lyafishiwa, lyafishiwa, lelo

Imfwa yandi, imfwa yandi, ndemufwila, ndemufwila, bonse

Kukufumya, kukufumya, ulubemba, ulubembu, lwenu.

Mulecita, mulecita kwibukisha, kwibukisha Ine.
