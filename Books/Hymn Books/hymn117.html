117. PAMO NA SHIKULU!

"For ever wish the Lord"

1. Pamo na Shikulu!

Ye, efyo nacibe:

E mweo wine ku mulu,

Ku bakwe beshibe.

Mu caloe mo ndi,

Ngo mweni ndelila

Lelo ndosha menso yandi

Ku mwesu kwashila.

2. Ku ng'anda kwa Tata,

Kwacishe fya panshi !

Ndelola fye ku cipata,

Ku mushi ku ntanshi.

Nabo wafuluka

Icalo nalumba,

Apa kwikala babuka,

'Bashila ba bumba.

3. Pamo na Shikulu !

Ye , Tata, mwasosa,

Ne shiwi 'li lituntulu

Kabili lyakosa,

Mwikale na ine,

Te kuti mfilule;

Mulendema, njise nine;

Mulelwa muntule.

4. Nga nkaleko mweo,

Nkapula mukati,

Pa kufwa ninshi mfyuke mfwa,

'Mweo naya sanga,

Lyene nkeshibisha,

Yangwe! 'fyo natemwa

Ishiwi 'li kusosa pe;

"Pe fye kuli Lesa!"

5. 'Kulile shiwi 'li,

Kwe penga lya mpela,

Kumfwikwa konse ku nshishi;

"Pe fye kuli Lesa!"

Lishiwi lya kwima,

Kupenda kwa kucimfya

Kabili, "Pe fye kwa Lesa!"

Amen, 'fyo fine.
