13. LUMBANYENI YESU UWATUUBULA

"Praise Him, Praise Him"

1. Lumbanyeni Yesu uwatulubula,

Imbisheni bonse ba panonse;

Celeleni Yesu, mwe bali mu mulu,

shina lipelwo bucindamo.

Nga kakumba, Yesu abaka bana,

Mu maboko alabasenda pe.

Lumbanyeni, lumbuleni ubukulu,

Mwimbileni no lwimbo pe na pe.

2. Lumbanyeni Yesu uwatulubula,

Aculile, apelelwe, afwa;

Wene libwe lyesu ilya bupususho,

Celeleni Yesu watanikwe.

Temwisheni Yesu watufwilile,

'Luse, congwe, no kutemwa kwa pe.

Lumbanyeni, lumbuleni ubukulu,

Mwimbileni ni lwimbo pe na pe.

3. Lumbanyeni Yesu uwatulubula,

NaHosana ku mulu kwaumfwa;

Yesu wne abelelyo kuteka,

Lumbanyeni kayula ne Mfumu.

Uwa kwisa e ukacimfya bonse

Alikwata maka no lulumbi.

Lumbanyeni lumbuleni ubukulu

Mwimbileni no lwimbo pe na pe.
