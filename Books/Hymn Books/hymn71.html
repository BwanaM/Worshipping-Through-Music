71. KULWA KWAPWA NO KUCULA

“The strife is o’er”

Aleluya! Aleluya! Aleluya!

1. Kulwa kwapwa no kuculo,

Mpalume yesu yalyansha,

Na bangeli balumbanye;

Aleluya!

2. Amaka ya mfwa yafilwa,

Pantu Kristu ayonaula,

Kubyo kupunda kwashila.

Aleluya!

3. Nshiku shitatu shapita,

Aimo Mumi ku bafwa,

Alumbwe ku ciyayaya,

Aleluya!

4. Afune fyela fye kumbo,

Aponya yonse mulando,

Embilwe pe ne sho nyimbo.

Aleluya!

4. Mwe Mfumu mwaliculishe,

Ku mfwa mwalitufumishe,

Twikale natumwimbile.

Aleluya!
