14. TUSEKELYO MWINE

"Rejoice, the Lord is King"

1. Tusekelyo Mwine

Ni Mfumu ya bantu,

Tumutotele fwe,

No kwimbile Mfumu;

Imye shiwi, no mutima,

Sekela no kusekela

2. Yesu ni Katula,

Aleteka bantu;

'Lintu atufwila

Anina ku mulu.

3. Ubufumu bwakwe

Tekuti bupwe yo;

Uluse akwata

Lwa mfwa no lwa kumbo.

4. Amaka 'kananye

Basuke bacimbe

Bonse abalwani;

Ku Mfumu balambe.

5. Imfumu ikesa

Ku kusenda nkuta,

Shiye kuli Lesa

Kwi sano lyashila
