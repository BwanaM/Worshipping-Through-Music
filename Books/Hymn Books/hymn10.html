10. INDIMI SHINGI NDEFWAYA

"O for a thousand tongues to sing"

1. Indimi shingi ndefwaya

Sha kwimbile Mfumu;

Ululumbi lwa kwa Lesa,

Luse ne cikuku.

2. Mwe Mfumu Lesa shikulu,

Ukwimba mungafwe;

No kushimikila kuno

Ifye shina lyenu.

3. Yesu, ishina lisuma,

Litamfye cikonko;

E lyawama ku bapipa,

Mutende no mweo.

4. Onaula maka yabi

Mubi akakula;

Amusamba no mulopa,

Kanshi ansambe 'ne.

5. Asosa, no kumfwe shiwi

Abafwa baima,

Basekela abakabi,

Baanga bapina.

6. Mumfwe, we wakoma matwe;

Cibulu, mulumbe;

We mpofu moneMfumwi yi;

Cilema ciluke.

7. Mwe bena fyalo moneni

Kuli Lesa wenu;

Muloleshe, mupusuke,

Mube ne cikuku.
