64. TULETASHO WATUFWILA

“We sing the praise of Him who died”

1. Tuletasho watufwila,

Wafwila bonse pa Muti;

Ne lyo abantu basula,

Cacisha fyonse fya panshi.

2. Pa Muti ulya palangwe

Cikuku cine ca Mfumu;

Asende membu sha ifwe,

Aleto luse lwa m’mulu

3. Umuti ! ufumyo bubi;

Ulepa mako wacula;

Usansamusha ku mfifi,

No kulowesha fyalula.

4. Ukosho m’tima wa kumfwa,

Ku kulwa e uleafwa;

Ufumyo mwenso wa mu mfwa,

Ubalikila balefwa.

5. Umuti e watwangasha;

Walango luse ulwa pa;

Ni umo mubi atusha;

Ne nkombe shimbe nshita pe.
