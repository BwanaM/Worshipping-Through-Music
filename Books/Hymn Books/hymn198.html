198. MWICITA IFIBI

"Do no sinful action"

1. Mwicita ifibi,

Mwikalipa fye;

Yesu Amuteke

Bana ba Mwine.

2. Kristu aliwama

Aletemwesha;

Bana bakwe nabo

Babe basuma.

3. 'Ciwa cilebepa

Cilafwaya pe;

Catila mubifye,

Mucinakile.

4. Lelo mwilatesha

Mwilanakila;

Loleni ku Mfumu,

Muleikonka.

5. Kristu aliwama

Aletemwesha;

Bana bakwe nabo

Babe basuma.
