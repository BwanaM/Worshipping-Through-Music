101. LESA WANDI NATOTELA

"Glory to Thee my God this night"

1. Lesa wandi natotela

mapalo yonse napelwa;

Munteke bwino bushiku

Ntushe mu kuboko kwenu.

2. Munjelele Mfumu yandi

Fyonse fyabipa nincite;

Kaba mu calo, na Imwe

Pa kulala mbyo mutende.

3. Tulumbanye Lesa wesu

E mutula palo lyesu,

'Ba mu calo, 'ba mu mulu

Lumbeni Lesa patatu.
