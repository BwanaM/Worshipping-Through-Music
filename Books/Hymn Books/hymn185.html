185. TE KUTI NKANE YESU YO

“I’m not ashamed to own my Lord”

1. Te kuti nkane Yesu yo,

Ndemucetekela;

Pa muti ancito mweo,

Ne shiwi lindela.

Pa muti, pa muti yashibulwa menso,

Ne cafina candi casendwa fye;

Mwandubwile, mwansenda no mwenso

Nomba mwampelo kusamwa pe.

2. Ni Yesu Mwine Katula,

Ishina ndelumba,

Tasebanya abakula

Kulya ku musumba.

3. Ilayano lyakwe lya cine

Kuti ambakila

Ico napele ku Mwine

Ku nshita yashila.

4. Akanumbula ne shina

Kuli Lesa Wishi,

No kunjikasho washina

Ku mwabo ku mushi.
