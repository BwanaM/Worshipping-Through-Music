5. SHIKULU E MFUMU; CALO

"The Lord is King"

1. Shikulu e Mfumu; calo

Cisekelele ne myulu,

Konse Konse cilumbuke

Ifyo Shikulu e Shifwe.

2. Shikulu e Mfumu; nani

Ulemukana ne fibi?

Nelyo Kumungwimgwintika,

No kukana musumina?

3. Shikulu e Mfumu; kanshi

Kapingula, Mupusushi;

Nshila shakwe e sha cine,

Abantu bese muwile.

4. Ateka; mwe basambi mwe,

Mwakulalumbanya Shifwe,

Na Yesu aba mu mulu,

Uwatanikwe ngo muntu.

5. Awe, teti apumbie,

Nelyo uluse lucepe,

Abena mwakwe beleka

Kwimbe fyo Shifwe ateka.
