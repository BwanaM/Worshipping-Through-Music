139. AFUMINE KU MULU

"Who came down from Heaven"

1. Afumine ku mulu,

Yesu Mulubushi,

Aishile ngo muntu,

Yesu Mulubushi.

Imbeni ulwimbo lu,

Ebeni bonsha bantu;

Afumine ku mulu,

Yesu Mulubushi.

2 Afwilile ku muti

Yesu Mulubushi,

Ku kutula na ine,

Yesu Mulubushi.

3 Mu mulu abe Mfumu,

Yesu Mulubushi,

Tunakile, tutemwe
Mulubushi

4 Ku mulu akekila

Yesu Mulubushi;

'Kututwala ku mwakwe,

Yesu Mulubushi.
