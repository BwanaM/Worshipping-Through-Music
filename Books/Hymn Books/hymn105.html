105. MWE TATA MUBASUMINISHE BEMININE PA NTANSHI

1. Mwe tata mubasuminishe beminine pa ntanshi yenu,

Sumineni bapokeleleni, sumineni babe benu mu cine.

2. Mwe Yesu aba bantu benu, Mwafwile pa kubalubula

Benu lelo, umfweni baleti twabika Imwe mu mitima yesu.

3. Na imwe Mupashi washila, 'seni nomba mwikale nabo,

Bafweni ukwenda bwino, umutende wisule mu mitima yabo,

4. Bacite milimo isuma, bapeleni bonse amaka,

'Kuti bende mubulungami basuke bafike kuli 'mwe ku mulu,

5. Mwe tata mutashiwe, na Imwe Yesu, Mulubushi wesu,

Na imwe Mupashi mu basangulule Lesa umo fye acindikwe.
