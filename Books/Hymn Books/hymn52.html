52. ISHIWI LYA MFUMU LILETI

1. Ishiwi lya Mfumu lileti, we fumye cili mu linso lyobe

Panuma walamona bwino mu linso lys mubiyo.

We munyina, we munyina, we fumye cili mu linso

Panuma walamona bwino mu linso lys mubiyo.

2. We kafinya balefumya cakutobola mu linso lyobe

Lyene walamona bwino mu linso lys mubiyo.

3. Lingikeninmitima yenu ku muntu umo na umo

Mwalaba icakumwenako nabo basumine.

4. Busumino ubwakweyeka ku mubiyo tabwilila

Tufumye ifyabipa fyesu elyo basumine.
