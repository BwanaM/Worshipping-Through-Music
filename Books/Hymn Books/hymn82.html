82. MWISE, MUPASHI BULESA

'Spirit divine, attend our prayers"

1. Mwise, Mupashi bu Lesa,

Iseni na maka;

Mube ne fwe mu ngande yi,

Fwe bantu banaka.

2. Mwise, Lubuto lwa, 'mulu,

Tumone 'fye cine;

Twende mu nshila ya mweo,

Umwaendo Mwine.

3. Mwise, Mulilo waoca,

Fipye fibi fyesu;

Tube capelwe cashila

Kwi shina lya Yesu.

4. Mwise, mwe Mume wabomfya,

Kuli 'fwe bauma,

Mumeshe no kufulisha

Fitwalo fisuma.

5. Mwise, mwe Nkunda yatemwa

,

No luse lwenu 'lu;

Bafwane 'benu ba panshi,

Nge fyo ba ku mulu.

6. Mwise, mwe Mwela wakuka,

Tube bapimpisha;

Na bana bonse ba bantu,

Babe abomfwisha.

7. Mwise, Mupashi wa mweo,

Wa cikuku conse;

Yesu alangwe ku bantu,

Babe 'bakwe bonse.
