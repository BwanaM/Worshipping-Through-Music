136. BULE SHINA LYA KWA YESU

"Take the Name of Jesus with you"

1. Bule shina lya kwa Yesu,

We walila, wacula;

Linasho bulanda bwesu,

Na ku fibi litula.

Ishina lyayemba!

tube na lyo, lyesu pe.

Ishina lyayemba!

tube na lyo, lyesu pe.

2. Bule shina lya kwa Yesu,

Libe 'nkwela yobe pe,

Ku nifwi ya m'lwani wesu,

Na ku fyonse fibepa.

3. Temwe shina lwakwa Yesu;

Lipelo mutende pe,

Libutusha mu nda shesu;

Liletwafwa twende pe.

4. Lumbe shina lya kwa Yesu;

Ku maboko apoka;

Yesu abo bumi bwesu;

Na ku lu se asoka.

5. Takalumbe shna, Yesu,

Nga twafika kwi sano

Ukuli ne Mfumu Yesu,

Lyasame fisamo.
