133. KATULA UMO WINE

"One offer of salvation to all the world"

1. Katula umo wine,

Wa bonse ba m'fyalo;

Ni Yesu kristu Mwine,

Lishinte ne Ntuo

Takuli limbi shina,

Takuli na shia,

Kano Kristu uwafina,

Watemwa, washila.

2. Ilambo limo line

Lya pa bubi bwesu,

Ku mwinshi na ko kumo,

Ne nshila ni Yesu.

3. Ku mwesu kwa pe kumo

Ukuli Ba Shifwe;

'Nasubi baba ba,o,

Kui Shikulwifwe.
