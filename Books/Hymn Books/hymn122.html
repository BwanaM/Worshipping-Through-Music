122. SEKELELENI! KALUBULA 'ISA

"Rejoice and be glad! The Redeemer has come"

1. Sekeleleni! Kalubula 'isa,

Tambeni mibele, e ya buLesa.

Cindikeni , lumbuleni uyo afwile,

Cindikeni, mulesamwa, atulubwile.

2. Sekeleleni! Kasuba katula;

Imfif ileya; fyapwe filufya.

3. Sekeleleni! Atumwene lambo;

Afute milandu, asendo lwambo

4. Sekeleleni! Abelelo lise;

Afumye fibifya , akushe ntuse,

5. Sekeleleni! Anashe filwani;

Atucimfishe mfwa; atupo mweo.

6. Sekeleleni! E uteka nomba;

Atusakamana no kulalomba.

7. Sekeleleni! Akesapingula;

Wa maka, wa ntaka, akalamula.
