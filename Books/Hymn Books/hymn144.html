144. YESU AITA BABI

"Sinners Jesus will receive"

1. Yesu aita babi,

abapokelela fye;

Bonse abalubile,

Bonse bangabwela fye

Imbileni, mwe bonse,

Yesu aita babi;

Ali no luse lonse,

Abapokelela fye.

2. Mwinse, mubyo mutende,

Eco ati, umfweni,

'Bubi bonse asende;

Mwe banaka iseni.

3. E usambo lubembu,

E uposho mutima;

Nomba ku cilye cakwe,

Teti ntine nakalya.

4. Yesu mweo wa babi,

No busuma ne cine;

Abaisa asambe,

Bakekala ku Mwine.
