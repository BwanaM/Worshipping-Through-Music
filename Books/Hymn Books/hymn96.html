96. MWE LESA WANDI, MUBE NA INE

"Abide with me"

1. Mwe Lesa wandi, mube na ine;

Yaise mfifi; mwise mwe Cine;

Takwaba kwafwa uko nkashine,

Wa kwafwa ni mwe, mube na ine.

2. Lulepwa bwangu lwendo lwesu 'lo;

'Fwa panshi fiya nge fya mu tulo;

Fyonse fyaluka, fintu namwenw;

Mwe bashaluka, mube na ine.

3. Mwali no luse kale kuli ne;

Ne lyo ne mubi nakaninine

Mwakano kunsha;cikuku cine:

Na ku ntashi fi mube na ine,

4. Ndefwaya Imwe, ndemukabila;

Ku bubi maka yenu yandwil;

Mumbaka, mwenshe, ku luse lwine;

Nomba bwaila, mube na ine.

5. Mwaba na ine te kuti ntine,

Na fyo ficusha te'ti fifine;

ne mfwa na maka ya bubi bwine

Nkacimfya, pantu mwaba na ine.

6. Lekeni mone ifyo mwafwile,

Na kumfwa yandi mumbalikile;

Bwaco bwa m'mulu, e bunsamine;

Ku mfwa, ku mweo, mube na ine.
