80. UWAFWIKILWE IMYUNGA

"The head that once was crowned with thorns"

1. Uwafwikilwe imyunga

Nomba alumbukwa,

Citebo cakwe cisuma

Ci;emusamika,

2. 'Cifolo cakwe ku mulu

Cili cakwe ceka,

'Mfumu ya sha Mfumu shonse,

'Lubuto ulwa pe.

3. 'Buseko bwa ba mu mulu,

Na ba panshi pamo,

'Luse lwakwe baishiba,

Ishina batemwa.

4. Lupanda lwakwe lwa nsoni,

'Cikuku caluko,

Lwabaletelo mutende

Ne nseko sha mweo.

5. Baculile Mfumu panshi,

Mu mulu bateka,

Ebo capelwo kwishiba

'Kutemwa kwakwe pe.

6. 'Lupanda kuli fwe bumi,

Ni mfwa kuli Yesu,

'Bucetekelobwa bantu,

'Landwelyabo lya pe.
