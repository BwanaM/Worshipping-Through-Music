213. MWE BAKACEMA MU MPANGA

"Gloria in Excelcis"

1. Mwe ba kacema mu mpanga

Mutwebw ifya bangeli

Bushe bamwebele shani

Bulya bushiku bwine?

Gloria in Excelsis Deo.

2. Twamwene cine cine

Lesa Mukulu mwine

Mu mubili wa buntunse

kanya kene mwi tanga.

3. Ba mu mulu baleimba

Umutende ku bantu

Fya mu mulu filelila

Icawamishe cibi.

4. Awe mukwai bakacema

Mwashimikisha cine

Tuye bonse tumumone

Lesa aisa kuli ifwe.
