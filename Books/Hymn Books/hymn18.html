18. PA BUSUMA BWA CALO

"For the beauty of the earth"

1. Pa busuma bwa calo,

Pa busuma bwa mulu,

Pa mulandu wa luse

Uli konse konse:

Shifwe kuli 'mwe lelo

Twimbyo lwimbo lwa nsek

2. Pa busuma bwa nshita

Sha bushiku kasabu,

Bwa mpili na manika,

Bwa miti na maluba:

Shifwe kuli 'mwe lelo

Twimbyo lwimbo lwa nsek

3. Panseko sha kutemwa

Kwa bafyashi na bana,

'Fibusa fya panonse

Ifyatwafwa mu luse:

Shifwe kuli 'mwe lelo

Twimbyo lwimbo lwa nsek

4. Pa mulandu wa bupe

Mutupela panonse,

Bonse ubutuntulu,

Bulafuma ku mulu:

Shifwe kuli 'mwe lelo

Twimbyo lwimbo lwa nsek

5. Pa Cilonganinwe ci,

Icipepa kuli 'mwe,

Cili mu fyalo fyonse,

Cilatasha kuli 'mwe:

Shifwe kuli 'mwe lelo

Twaimbyo lwimbo lwa nse
