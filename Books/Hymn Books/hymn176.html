176. MFISA MWE MFUMU MWE BENE

“Hide me, O my savior”

1. Mfisa mwe Mfumu, mwe bene.

Mu lwafyo lwenu,

Lubuto lobe lumfimbe,

Nkumone pa menso.

Mfisa, mfisa

We Mupusushi mfisa,

We Mfumu ndama

Mfisame muli ‘we

2. Icipupu canyimina,

Kuno uko ndi,

Mpisha ku mulu waciko,

Nsumbulwe kuli ;we.

3. ‘Musana nga wafunika

Mu kuculisha,

Pa kukuta, “Mfumu ngafwa,”

We mwine ngasuka.

4. Mfisa, Mfumu napapata,

Nshili na maka,

We wa maka Mupusushi,

Ni we mulubushi.
