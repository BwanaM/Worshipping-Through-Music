132. NA LIMBI 'IYO WE MUTIMA WANDI

"Once more my soul"

1. Na limbi iyo we mutima wandi,

Shiwi lya Mpusushi;

'Mfumu nomba leka ntale mone,

Bushe musubile?

Eya; eya; Lesa njafweniko

Kuba wenu!

Mwanshita mu mulopa wafina

Wya! ndeba wenu.

2. Mu cikuku emo mpokelela

Kutemwa kwenu 'ku;

Muli Kristu e mo ndesubila,

Mucetekela pe.

3. Mwaishibe fyo nshikwete maka

Ndatina ukuwa,

Nimpenya maka mutumikile,

Yenu yefulisha.

4. Na nomba Mfumu twamupapata

Mubafwe 'ba bonse

Kuti bembe no mutina umo,

"Eya! ndeba wenu."

5. Kale bonse abamulombele

Abati, "Mutwafye,"

Bwangu bwangu, "Eya!" mwabaswike

Na kuli fwe nomba.
