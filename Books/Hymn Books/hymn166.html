166. PEPENI LESA MU MUSHILO WAYEMBA

" O worship the Lord in the beauty of holiness"

1. Pepeni Lesa mu mushilo wayemba;

Toteni ukwali, muletina;

Mwise ne cumfwila cine cishilemba,

Mufuke; pantu Lesa lishina.

2. Ifisakamina bikeni ukwali;

Wa maka wa luse alesenda,

Aleafwa no kubaka ku sha nkali,

Alecita cisuma we wenda.

3. Wiba no mwenso pantu walisaluka,

Tasale cinso tapende fyuma;

Icine cayemba no luse lwafuka,

E fya Lesa 'ti fisuma.

4. Nga twatule fi, atemwa kufimona;

Alepa fwe balanda ukwanga;

Ku batutuma na batine fipona,

Amano no luse alelanga.

5. Pepeni Lesa mu mushilo wayemba;

Toteni ukwali, muletina;

Mwise ne cumfwila cine cishilemba,

Mufuke; pantu Lesa lishina.
