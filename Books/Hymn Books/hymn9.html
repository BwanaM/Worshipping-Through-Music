9.FIBUMBWA FYONSE FYA PANSHI

"All creatures of our God and King"

1. Fibumbwa fyonse fya panshi

Aleni, twimbile Mfumu,

Aleluya, Aleluya,

We kasuba wabalika,

Nobe we mwenshi bushiku,

Mulumbanye, mulumbanye;

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya.

2. We mwela wa cipupu we,

We kumbi lyayo lubilo,

Mulumbanye, Aleluya,

Bwaca buletasha Mfumu,

Myengelele, imye shiwi,

Mulumbanye, mulumbanye;

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya.

3. Mumana we mu kupita,

Bulo lwimbo no kutasha

Mfumu Lesa, shilimbeni,

Mulilo wa kwafwa bantu,

No kubapelo lubuto,

Imye shiwi mulumbanye

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya

4. We mayo calo nshiku pe

Watupe fyakulya fyonse,

Ulwimbo mwimbileni,

Fimuti na maluba, mwe,

Langeni lulumbi lwakwe,

Toteleni Mfumu Lesa;

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya.

5. Abatemwana mu bantu,

Abelelana, imbeni,

Temwisheni Mfumu yenu.

Na balecula nshiku pe

Basansamuke babula

Shiwi ili lya kutota;

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya.

6. We mfwa yaisa Kuli fwe,

Mu kutalala, shiili,

Tatutina, we munensu,

Niwe nkombe ya kwa Tata,

Wasendo mwana ku mwabo,

We mfwa mune, we mutembo;

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya

7. Fibumbwa fyonse fya panshi

Filumbanye no mucinshi

Lesa wesu, Mfumu yesu.

Tasheni Tata no Mwana,

Mupashi nao Wabuta;

Lumbanyeni, batasheni;

Aleluya, Aleluya, Aleluya
