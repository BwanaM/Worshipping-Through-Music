47. YESU, 'YU MWANA LESA, YESU

"Jesus Son of God"

1. Yesu, 'yu Mwana Lesa, Yesu,

'Mulop twasambwa, ifwe,

Mwikale ne fwe, 'mpulumushi shonse

Tupose, Yesu Kristu

2. Lesa Tata, Yesu Mwana wenu

Afwililya bantu bonse,

Tumulumbanye, Yesu e katula,

Wa maka yonse, no luse.

3. 'Mfumu yesu, tuli mu mfifi fye,

Pa kutemwe fya mu calo

Twaliluba fye, mutukkule

Mu minyololo, tuli basha.

4. Mu mafunde, Yesu atupele,

Ati: "senda Landwe lyandi

"Mu calo conse, ndemupa maka

Kubwesha bonse, kuli ine."
