208. FYAPYA MWI BALA

"The fields are all white"

1. Fyapya mwi bala,

Na basepa bace,

Tulecita shani,

Tuli baice,

Ku kwafwe mfumu ukusepa?

2. Shacepe ndupi,

Ne fyebo fyanaka,

Twafilwo kubomba;

Tuli na maka,

Bushe, kuyafwa ukusep?

3. Twakulapepa,

No kupele fyesu;

Twakulaikanya,

No kumfwa Yesu;

E kwafwe Mfumu ukusepa.

4. Ilyo twakula

Tukaba basepa,

'Batemwo kutumwa,

Nelyo kwalepa,

Ku kwafwe mfumu ukusepa.
