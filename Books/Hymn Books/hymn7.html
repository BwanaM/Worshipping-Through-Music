7. TUCINDIKE NO KWANGA

"Let us with a gladsome mind"

1. Tucindike no kwanga,

Lesa wesu musuma

Aba no luse lwa pe

Pa pantu bakwe bonse.

2. Tulumbule ishina,

Ni Lesa Mwine eka.

3. Abumbile icalo,

No kwisusho lubuto.

4. Musuma Kuli bonse

E ulelela fyone.

5. Alebatangilila

Bantu bakwe mu nshila.

6. Atumona no luse,

Fwe balanda, fwe ba fye.

7. Tulelumba na nomba

Lesa wesu musuma.
