61. OWE, OWE MWE BANTU UMFWENI SHIWI

1. Owe, owe mwe bantu umfweni shiwi

Elya soswe lelo lyafika mu mitima

Lolekesha pa lupanda, mone Mfumu.

2. Owe, owe Kanshi ni pali ine

Mfumu yandi yaculile, yamfwilile

Pa bubi ubo ncita, ubo ncita.

3. Owe, owe filamba fyaisula mens0

Ndelila pa nkasa sha kwa Yesu,

Andubwile ne mubi, ndi mubi cine

4. Owe, owe tekuti twishibishe

Uyu mulandu wakwa Yesu wakwa uwacita,

No luse no kutemwa pali ifwe

5. We katula, Mulubushi, naisa kuli iwe,

Unjibukishe lintu uli mu bufuma,

Napapata we Mwana wakwa Lesa.
