44. SHIWI LYAIKALA

"Lord, Thy word abideth"

1. Shiwi lyaikala,

Litutungula;

Untu asumina,

'Nseko alepelwa.

2. Nga babi baliko,

Ltupo buseko;

Litusansamusha,

Liletupususha

3. 'Lyo bulanda bwisa,

Na ifwe twatunkwa,

Liletufundisha,

Litubakilila.

4. Uwishibe nani,

Ubuseko bwe shiwi,

Buntu tulepelwa,

Fwe balesumina?

5. 'Fwe kuti twishibe

Fyonse litufundusha,

Kuti tumutemwe,

No kwikala nemwe.

6. Ishiwi lya luse

Lyafwa aba mweo;

Ishiwi lya mweo

Lyafwa abalefwa.
