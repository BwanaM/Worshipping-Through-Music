17. NE SESE NE PENGA 'BENGI BALIKO

"With harps and with viols"

1. Ne sese ne penga 'bengi baliko,

Na mu cinso ca Mfumu baimbo lwimbo

Kuli uyo watutemwa, watulubula,

Kuli wenya lulumbi inshiku shonse.

2. Abalya babipa bali mu cinso,

Balifwikwe fyabuta no kwimba pamo:

3. Acita bapipa babe ba Mfumu,

Atutula, twafundwa, twimbyo lwimbo

.

4. Twali fye mu mfifi, twali abash

Lelo alitusamba, twasangululwa;

5. Tumulumbanishe Imfumu Yesu,

'Kuti bambi pa kumfwa bamwimbilyo
