78. APWE PENGA LYA KWA LESA

When the roll is called up yonder

1. Apwe penga lya kwa Lesa likomfwikwa mu calo,

Apo bukaca bwa lubuto tu tu,

Apo abasalwa bakaya longana pe shilya

Apwekete na mashina, nkabeko.

Apwakete na mashina,

Apwakete na mashina,

Apwakete na mashina,

Apwakete na mashina nkabeko

2. Ubushiku bwa kutinya apwa bafwa bakema,

No kumono bukankala bwa kwa Kristu;

Apo abasalwa bakaya longana kwa Lesa,

Apwakete na mashina nkabeko.

3. Aleni tulebombela Shikullwifwe nshiku pe,

Tuye landa ku bantu ifya mweo,

Nge milimo yapwa, ne few twaya mu calo cilia,

Apwakete na mashina nkabeko.
