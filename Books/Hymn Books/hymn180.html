180. IFYO NDI, -NSHITELEBWILA

"Just as I am”

1. Ifyo ndi, -nshitelebwila

Umulopa wansimune

Kuli imwe, mwe banjita,

Mwe Mfumu, naisa.

2. Ifyo ndi,-nshilepembela

Kufumya cimo cabipa,

Kulim mwe, mwe ba kunsamba

Mwe Mfumu, naisa.

3. Ifyo ndi,-mwalinjishiba,

Napenampena ne fingi,

Kulwishanya mu mutima,

Mwe Mfumu, naisa.

4. Ifyo ndi-‘mupina, ‘mopfu,

Kampoke fyuma fya mpomfu;

Ukufisanga ku mwenu;

Mwe Mfumu, naisa.

5. Ifyo ndi-mwampokelela,

Kunjelela no kunsamba,

Fya mwalaya nasumina,

Mwe Mfumu, naisa.

6. Ifyo ndi-‘kutemwa kwenu,

Kwayanshe filwani bwangu,

Nomba, mwe mfumu, ndi wenu,

Mwe Mfumu, naisa.
