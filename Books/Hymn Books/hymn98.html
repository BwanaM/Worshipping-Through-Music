98. AKASUBA KAWA

"Now the day is over"

1. Akasuba kawa,

Nomba bwaila;

'Milimo naipwa,

'Bantu batusha.

2. Nomba kuli mfifi,

'Ntanda shabeka,

Inama ne fyuni

Filesandema.

3. 'Banaka bapelwo

Tulo tusuma;

No bupalo bwenu

Tuleshibata.

4. Mupele abana

Bamumone 'mwe;

Mutule bacoba

Ba pa cambeshi.

5. Mutekye balwele

Abakalipwa;

Abacite fibi

Mulebelela.

6. No bushiku buno

'Bangeli bene

Batubaka bwino,

Batupempula.

7. Ngo bushiku bwaca

Mukanjimye'mwe,

'Mutima wabuta,

Mpalana ne mwe.

8. Tulumbanye Lesa,

Wishi na Mwane,

Mupashi Mushilo,

Nshiku shonse pe.
