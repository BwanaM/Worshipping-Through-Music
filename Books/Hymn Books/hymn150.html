150. MU BUSHIKU BWA BULANDA

"Through the night of doubt and sorrow"

1. Mu bushiku bwa bulanda

Abanyendo balaya,

Balalumba no kuloba

Kuntu kwabo walaya.

2. Kulya kwabo kubekesha

Ni Kasomoku ntanshi;

Abe senge bakoshanya,

Benda bashipa nganshi.

3. Lumo lwasho lwa kwa Lesa

Ku lukuta lwashila;

E lutamfye fingatinya,

E lulanga ne nshila.

4. Cimo ico baendela

Ne subilo lyakosa,

Kumo kwine kulolela

Kuli ifyo Lesa 'sosa.

5. Lumo lwimbo lu lwa kwanga

Lwa kwa Shibo na Mwane;

Lumo lwendo no kupanga,

Kumo kwesha bayane.

6. Kumo kwanga nga bafika

Ku musumba mo wa pe;

Shibo umo ekwateka,

Uli no luse lwe pe.
