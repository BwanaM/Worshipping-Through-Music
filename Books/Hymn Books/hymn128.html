128. ONSE UTEMENWE! BOMFWE BONSE

"Whosoever will"

1. ''Onse utemenwe! bomfwe bonse"

Tumeni imbila mu calo conse!

Lekeni beshibe abantu bonse;

"Utemenwe engesa!"

Onse watemwa! onse watemwa!

Bileni monse mu mpili ne nika;

Uwito waluba ni Shifwe Lesa;

"Utemenwe engesa!"

2. Onse uleisa ecelwako;

E kuli no mwinshu, waisulwako:

Ne nshila ya mweo yene yabako:

"Utemenwe engesa!"

3. "Onse utemenwe" Lesa 'bila;

"Onse utemenwe" cebo cashila;

"Onse utemenwe" mweo wacila:

"Utemenwe engesa!"
