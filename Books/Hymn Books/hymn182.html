182 BUNO BUSHIKU KUSHUKA

“O happy day!”

1. Buno bushiku kushuka

Apo nasanga katula;

‘Mutima wandi wayanga;

Ndebeba fye ‘bantu bonse.

‘Kushuka! ‘Kushuka!

Apo ansamba ku fibi!

No kumfundisho kupepa

No kunsekele nshiku pe;

‘Kushuka! ‘Kushuka!

Apo ansamba ku fibi!

2. ‘Mulandu onse upwile!

Ne wa kwa Yesu, wafutwa;

Ankula ine no luse

Na ine nomba ndekonka.

3. Utushe fye we mutima

Wifuma ku Mfumu yobe;

Sango mutende lwa Lesa;

Fisuma fyonse ukwete.

4. Lesa aumfwe layano,

Akulaumfwe nshiku pe,

Isuke imfwa ikese,

Nkalumbe Mfumu ya luse.
