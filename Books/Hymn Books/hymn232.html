232. MUPALE CI CALO

“God bless our native land”

1. Mupale ci calo,

Amaka ya mulu

Yacibaka.

‘Mutende ube pe,

Abantu baleke

Ukulwe nshiku pe;

Mucipale

2. Mubake shamfumu,

Ubumu bwa mulu

Mushipele.

Shibe sha mutende,

Shange, shisekele,

Shiteke no luse,

Mushipale.

3. Mutule abantu,

Batemwe Kristu,

Babe bakwe.

Bafume mu mfifi,

Bakonke Mfumwi yi,

Mubatule
