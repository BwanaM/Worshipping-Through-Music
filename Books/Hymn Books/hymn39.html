39. LYA KUTEMWO KUCILA

"Jesus, Name of wonderous love"

1. Lya kutemwo kucila

Ili shina lyabila;

Kwi shina lya kwa Yesu

Twinike 'mitwe yesu.

2. Yesu 'shina lyashiila

Ku baluba mu nshila;

Na ku bantu bacula

Wene e ukatula.

3. Yesu 'shina lyawama

'Lya waisatulama;

Waisa mu bucushi

Abe fi Mupusushi.

4. Yesu, lishina lyeka

Mu mashina yaseka,

Ilyo lyatulula

Ku bubi ubwakula.

5. Yesu! e lya kutemwa,

Umo buntu bwalemwa;

Yesu! e lyo lyawama,

Emo tulebelama.
