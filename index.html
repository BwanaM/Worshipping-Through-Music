About Worshipping Through Music

Worshipping Through Music is dedicated to preserving and promoting traditional Christian music, especially Bemba hymns and Inyimbo Sha Bwina Christu. We believe that music is a powerful tool for worship that connects us to God and to our cultural heritage.

Our mission is to make these spiritual resources accessible to everyone, whether through digital music videos or physical hymn books. We serve communities across Zambia and beyond, helping people deepen their worship experience through meaningful music.
